Aaron Charlton, PhD, MBA
Assistant Professor of Marketing at Illinois State University

I teach marketing research and marketing communications. Students in my classes work with real-world clients in order to gain hands-on experience. There was no book for this sort of thing so I wrote the book (and it's free): Real World Clients: A Guide for Student Consultants.

My research is focused on understanding and improving the practice of scientific research within the field of marketing. I run the Metascience & Marketing Lab at ISU and I created OpenMKTG.org, a website aimed at increasing transparency and quality in marketing science.
